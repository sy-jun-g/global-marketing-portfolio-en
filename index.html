03
Newsletter Launch & Growth
Contribution: 100%
Content Strategy✦ LLM ToolsB2CEmail Marketing
Background

The company had no direct communication channel with its global audience. Previous newsletter attempts were product-heavy and generated little engagement — audiences had no reason to subscribe to a brand they didn't know.

Goal

Build a newsletter from zero that people actually want to read, then use it to gradually introduce the company's products and positioning.

Approach

Pivoted from product-push to value-first: weekly curated cybersecurity news the target audience genuinely cares about. Used LLM Tools to identify trending topics, summarize complex news, and refine tone at speed. Occasionally featured company news within the same issue.

Lesson Learned

Audiences subscribe to what helps them, not what sells to them. Once trust is built through consistent value delivery, brand content becomes something they welcome rather than skip.
